# ✈️ Airline Passenger Satisfaction Dashboard

## 1. Project Title / Headline

### ✈️ Airline Passenger Satisfaction Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze airline passenger demographics, travel patterns, flight performance, and customer satisfaction metrics to support data-driven decision-making and service quality improvement.

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Airline Passenger Satisfaction Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report developed to analyze passenger satisfaction and travel behavior across a large airline dataset. The dashboard enables users to monitor key operational metrics, explore passenger demographics, evaluate customer satisfaction levels, and identify factors that influence the overall travel experience. This dashboard is intended for airline management, business analysts, customer experience teams, and decision-makers seeking actionable insights into passenger preferences and service performance.

---

## 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• **Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used for dashboard development and report creation.<br>
• **Power Query** – Used for data preparation, transformation, and cleaning processes.<br>
• **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used to create calculated measures, KPIs, and satisfaction metrics.<br>
• **Bookmarks & Navigation** – Implemented to create dynamic views between overall ratings and detailed service ratings.<br>
• **Interactive Visualizations** – KPI Cards, Clustered Column Charts, Stacked Column Charts, Stacked Bar Charts, Donut Charts, Gauge Charts, Slicers, and Navigation Buttons.<br>
• **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard preview.

---

## 4. Data Source

**Source:** Airline Passenger Satisfaction Dataset

The dataset contains over 1.8 million passenger records, including demographic information, customer type, travel class, flight distance, departure delays, arrival delays, travel purpose, satisfaction status, and service ratings. The data was utilized to analyze passenger behavior, travel experiences, and overall customer satisfaction.

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

### 📌 Business Problem

Airlines collect large amounts of customer and operational data, but extracting meaningful insights from raw datasets can be challenging. Understanding passenger satisfaction, service quality, and travel behavior is essential for improving customer experience and maintaining competitive advantage.

Key questions addressed include:

- What percentage of passengers are satisfied with their overall travel experience?
- How does satisfaction vary across customer segments?
- Which travel classes attract the most passengers?
- How do passenger demographics influence travel patterns?
- What impact do flight delays have on customer experience?
- How do service ratings contribute to overall satisfaction?

---

### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard aims to:

- Monitor key passenger and operational metrics through KPI cards.
- Analyze passenger demographics and customer segments.
- Evaluate satisfaction levels across different passenger groups.
- Compare travel behavior by class, customer type, and travel purpose.
- Explore service quality ratings and identify improvement opportunities.
- Provide an interactive reporting experience using bookmarks and slicers.

### 📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals

#### 📌 Landing Page

Provides dashboard navigation and user-friendly access to analysis pages.

---

#### 📌 Passenger Overview Page

##### KPI Cards

Displays important airline metrics including:

- Total Passengers
- Average Age
- Average Departure Delay
- Average Arrival Delay
- Average Flight Distance

##### Passengers by Gender and Customer Type

A clustered column chart compares passenger distribution across genders and customer categories.

##### Passengers by Travel Class

A donut chart visualizes passenger distribution across travel classes.

##### Overall Satisfaction Percentage

A gauge chart displays the percentage of satisfied passengers against total passengers.

---

#### 📌 Passenger Experience Page

##### Passengers by Age Group and Customer Type

A stacked column chart analyzes customer segments across different age groups.

##### Passenger Satisfaction Analysis

A stacked bar chart compares satisfied and dissatisfied passengers.

##### Service Ratings Analysis

Bookmark functionality allows users to switch between:

- Overall Service Ratings
- Detailed Service Ratings

This provides both high-level and detailed insights into passenger experience and service performance.

##### Interactive Filters

Slicers enable dynamic filtering by:

- Customer Type
- Travel Class
- Type of Travel

### 💡 Business Impact & Insights

- **Passenger Segmentation:** Enables deeper understanding of customer demographics and travel behavior.
